What is Pension Credit?

Pension Credit is an income-related benefit for people over State Pension age on low income. It tops up your weekly income to a guaranteed minimum level and can provide extra for savings, disability, or caring responsibilities.

  • Guarantee Credit (2025/26): Tops up income to £227.10 single / £346.60 couple per week.
  • Savings Credit: Extra for modest savings (available if reached State Pension age before 6 April 2016).
  • Average award: Around £3,900 per year.
  • Eligibility basics: Over State Pension age, low income, UK resident, savings generally under £10,000 for full amount.
  • Extras: Severe disability premium, carer premium, housing costs help.

Success-based fee from £99 only if you win (often covered by backpayments).
