About Claim For Me

Dedicated to helping people over State Pension age with benefit applications

Who We Are

Expert, Caring Support for Benefits Applications

Claim For Me was created because we know first-hand how difficult and stressful the benefits system can be — especially when you’re dealing with illness, caring for a loved one, or supporting an elderly parent.

Around ten years ago, when my dad became seriously ill, I was responsible for helping him apply for benefits such as Attendance Allowance and Carer’s Allowance. At a time when our family was already under emotional strain, we also had to deal with long forms, complicated questions and constant worry about whether we had filled everything in correctly.

Like many people, I remember the stress of waiting weeks for a response, only to have forms sent back because something wasn’t completed exactly right. Each delay meant more uncertainty, more chasing, and more pressure at a time when our focus should have been on my dad, not paperwork.

What stood out to me most was the constant “what if” — what if we’ve missed something, what if we’ve said the wrong thing, what if this causes delays, and what if support is delayed.

Sadly, this is still the reality for many families today.

That personal experience is what led to the creation of Claim For Me.

We exist to take that stress away. We help older people and their families with applications for Attendance Allowance, Pension Credit and Blue Badge, making sure forms are completed properly and clearly.

Our service is designed to be straightforward, supportive and respectful. We understand that behind every application is a real person, a real family and often a difficult situation.

If you’re caring for someone, or helping a loved one, you shouldn’t have to struggle with complex systems and paperwork on your own.

That’s why we’re here.

Our experienced team specialises in Attendance Allowance, Pension Credit, and Blue Badge applications. We handle everything from the initial eligibility check to submission and follow-up.

We operate on a success-based basis – there are no upfront costs, and our fee is only payable if your application succeeds (often covered by backdated payments).
