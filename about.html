Free Eligibility Check

Quick 4-step check to see if you may qualify for Attendance Allowance, Pension Credit or Blue Badge

Takes less than 2 minutes • No personal details saved • No obligation

1. Are you at State Pension age or older?

Current State Pension age is 66 (rising in future years).

2. Do you need help or supervision with personal care?

This includes washing, dressing, using the toilet, eating, or needing someone to keep an eye on you due to illness/disability (Attendance Allowance key question).

3. Is your weekly income low?

Rough guide for Pension Credit: below ~£201/week (single) or ~£307/week (couple) after housing costs. This is approximate – we can check accurately.

4. Do you have mobility difficulties?

Do you have severe walking problems, need help to move around, or have a condition that affects your ability to walk safely? (Key for Blue Badge)
