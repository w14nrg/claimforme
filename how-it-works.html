How It Works

Our simple, stress-free 6-step process

Everything is done over the phone – no complicated forms for you to fill in.
No upfront fees • You only pay if you win

1

Complete Your Free Eligibility Questionnaire

Choose your benefit and answer a few quick questions online. It only takes minutes and helps us confirm if you're likely eligible – completely free and no obligation.

2

We Call You to Arrange Your Appointment

One of our friendly agents will contact you promptly to discuss your questionnaire results and book a convenient phone appointment with a benefits expert. We'll let you know exactly what information you'll need.

3

Expert Phone Consultation

Your dedicated Claim For Me expert will call at the agreed time. They'll guide you through everything needed for your claim, answering any questions and gathering details accurately – all from the comfort of your home.

4

We Prepare & Submit Your Claim

Our experts complete your full application with all the right details to maximise success, then submit it to the relevant authority (DWP or local council) on your behalf.

5

Decision & Award

The authority reviews your claim (processing times vary). We handle any follow-ups if needed. Successful claims often include backdated payments.

6

Receive Your Benefits & Settle Our Fee

You start receiving your regular payments (and any backdated amount). You only pay our success fee at this stage – flexibly via standing order or invoice, whichever suits you best.

What You'll Need for Your Phone Consultation

Don't worry – our expert will guide you through everything, and you can have a family member or carer with you on the call. It's helpful (but not essential) to have the following ready:

  • Your National Insurance number
  • Your address and contact details
  • Details of your disability or health condition (and how it affects daily living)
  • Details of your GP surgery or medical centre
  • Details of any care home, hospital, or hospice you're currently staying in (if applicable)
  • Details of any other benefits you're currently receiving

We handle all the paperwork – you just need to chat with us!

No win, no fee – you only ever pay if your claim succeeds,
and it's usually covered by your backdated award.
